About Alpine Trekking Trails

These legendary trails weave through ancient pine forests, meadows sprinkled with wildflowers, and narrow ridgelines offering endless mountain views. Every step rewards you with nature's raw beauty and the thrill of discovery.

Why Visit?

  • Multiple trekking routes for beginners to pro hikers
  • Uncharted spots for offbeat adventure videos
  • Authentic homestays and local delicacies along the way

Best Time to Trek

March to June and September to November are ideal. Avoid mid-winter months as heavy snow blocks some routes, unless you're up for snow treks!
